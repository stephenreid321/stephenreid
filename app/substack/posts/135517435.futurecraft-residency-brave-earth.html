Trinity of Values

  • Regeneration: Implementing regenerative systems for healing, renewal, and restoration

  • Resiliency: Creating interconnected, diverse, networks of solidarity and mutual support

  • Reciprocity: Cultivating a relational field of symbiotic enrichment

Trinity of Inquiry

  • Gnosis: Drawing from all wisdom traditions to establish a direct relationship to the divine

  • Eros: Embodied practice cultivating a relationship with that which enlivens

  • Polis: Refining our relationship to the whole through systems that promote equity and sovereignty
