Platform Co-ops Now!

I’ve started studying on the Platform Co-ops Now! course, a 3-month “practical and theoretical online program to learn about and incubate platform co-ops” where I will be seeking to develop the Dandelion web platform as a not-for-profit co-operative alternative to corporate ticketing platforms such as Eventbrite.

The course is a collaboration between the Institute for the Cooperative Digital Economy at the New School (NY, USA) and Mondragon University (a Basque not-for-profit co-operative university, part of Mondragon, one of the largest worker co-ops in the world with 80,000+ workers). Read more

Begin your task before it becomes a burden
Put things in order before they get out of hand
Remember,
A tree that fills a man’s embrace grows from a seedling
A tower nine stories high starts with one brick
A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step

Act and it’s ruined
Grab and it’s gone
People on the verge of success often lose patience
and fail in their undertakings

and you won’t bring on failure
